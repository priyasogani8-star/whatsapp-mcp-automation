# 📱 Simple User Guide - How to Use Polls

## For Regular People (Not Developers)

---

## 🎯 What Can I Do With This?

Imagine you're in a WhatsApp group and you want to:
- Vote on where to eat lunch
- Choose which property to buy
- Pick the best movie to watch
- Select your skills for a job

**This tool makes all of that easy!**

---

## 🗳️ Two Types of Voting

### Type 1: Single-Choice (Pick 1)

```
Question: "Where should we eat?"

🍕 Pizza Place
🍣 Sushi Bar  
🍔 Burger King

You pick: 🍣 Sushi Bar

Later, you change your mind to: 🍕 Pizza Place
(Your Sushi vote disappears, Pizza vote appears)
```

**Use this when:** You need ONE answer
- Pick ONE restaurant
- Choose ONE time for meeting
- Pick ONE color
- Vote YES or NO

---

### Type 2: Multiple-Choice (Pick Many)

```
Question: "What property types interest you?"

☑ 1 BHK
☑ 2 BHK
☐ 3 BHK
☑ 4 BHK
☐ Villa

You pick: 1 BHK, 2 BHK, AND 4 BHK (all three!)

Later, you also want: Villa
(Now you have: 1 BHK, 2 BHK, 4 BHK, Villa)

Later, you remove: 2 BHK
(Now you have: 1 BHK, 4 BHK, Villa)
```

**Use this when:** You want MULTIPLE options
- Pick multiple properties you like
- Select multiple skills you have
- Choose multiple dietary preferences
- Pick multiple features you want

**Scenario:** Family looking for apartment

## 🚦 Rules For Each Type

### Single-Choice Rules:
✅ **Can do:**
- Pick exactly 1 option
- Change to a different option later
- See all votes counted

❌ **Cannot do:**
- Pick 0 options (you must pick something)
- Pick 2+ options (only 1 allowed)

### Multiple-Choice Rules:
✅ **Can do:**
- Pick 1, 2, 3, or more options (depending on what creator set)
- Add more selections later
- Remove selections later  
- Change all selections at once

❌ **Cannot do:**
- Pick fewer than minimum required
- Pick more than maximum allowed
- Pick same option twice

## 📊 How to Read Results

```
Poll Results:

Pizza Place: 5 votes (50%)
Sushi Bar: 3 votes (30%)
Burger King: 2 votes (20%)

What does this mean?

Total people who voted: 5 + 3 + 2 = 10 people
- 50% of votes went to Pizza (that's 5 out of 10)
- 30% went to Sushi (that's 3 out of 10)
- 20% went to Burger (that's 2 out of 10)

Winner: Pizza Place! 🎉
```

## 💬 Common Questions

### Q: Can I see who voted for what?
**A:** Not usually - votes are private. But the total numbers are public.

### Q: Can I change my vote multiple times?
**A:** Yes! Change as many times as you want.

### Q: What if I pick a wrong option?
**A:** Just change your vote! It's super easy.

### Q: What's the difference between single and multiple again?
**A:**
- **Single:** Pick ONE (like choosing a restaurant)
- **Multiple:** Pick MULTIPLE (like choosing properties you like)

### Q: How many votes can I give?
- **Single:** 1 vote (that's it!)
- **Multiple:** Between 1 and max (decided by creator)

### Q: Can the creator vote?
**A:** Yes! Everyone votes the same way.

### Q: When does the poll end?
**A:** When the creator closes it, or on the expiry date they set.

- "What properties interest you?" → Pick ALL interested

- "What languages do you speak?" → Pick ALL spoken
